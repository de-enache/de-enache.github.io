UNIT 5700 (Ongoing)

The Wolf in 90's Clothing

Project Overview

The UNIT 5700 represents the pinnacle of silent computing engineering. Built with the singular vision of creating a workstation capable of medium to heavy AI inference and local development while remaining completely stealth silent during idle operations and whisper-quiet under full load.

Noise Targets: 0dB at idle, <25dB at load

Thermal Engineering

  • Cooling Solution: ID-COOLING premium tower cooler
  • Custom Curves: Optimized fan speed profiles
  • Airflow Design: Balanced intake/exhaust configuration
  • Acoustic Damping: Strategic placement of noise-dampening materials
  • Temperature Control: Advanced BIOS tuning for stability

Performance Characteristics

The system is specifically optimized for Ryzen 7 5700X high-clock stability, allowing for sustained performance during intensive AI inference tasks without thermal throttling. Custom fan curves ensure that noise remains imperceptible during typical development workloads while ramping up efficiently only when necessary.

Use Cases
  • AI Inference: Local LLM deployment and experimentation
  • Development: Heavy compilation and multi-threaded workflows
  • 24/7 Operation: Silent background processing without distraction
  • Content Creation: Video editing, rendering, and media processing
